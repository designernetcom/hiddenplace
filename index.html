Hidden Place is Pune’s favourite watering hole.We’re spreading our vibes in Koregaon Park & More on its way.

All over the world, in every little town, there is a place where the thirsty find sanctuary. Popularly known as a tavern, watering hole, corner pub or just a hang-out,this place brings friends together after a long day’s work to just have a few drinks and un-wind a little. Everyone has a story to share, some happy, some sad.

Cheer for your favourite team and drink to its victory or defeat. At the end of the evening all who leave the place go home feeling like they’re on top of the world.
